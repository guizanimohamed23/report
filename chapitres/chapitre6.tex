\section{AI Integration}

CyberScope integrates an artificial intelligence layer to enhance vulnerability analysis and provide higher-level insights beyond raw technical findings.

The AI module is designed to process scan results and generate meaningful interpretations, including risk evaluation, prioritization of vulnerabilities, and contextual understanding of potential attack scenarios.

\subsection{Risk Assessment}

The system analyzes detected vulnerabilities and evaluates their severity based on multiple factors, including CVSS scores, affected endpoints, and potential impact.

This allows CyberScope to provide an overall risk score for the scanned application and guide users toward the most critical issues.

\subsection{Attack Scenario Generation}

For high-risk vulnerabilities, the system generates potential attack scenarios describing how an attacker could exploit the identified weaknesses.

These scenarios help users better understand the real-world implications of vulnerabilities and support decision-making in remediation strategies.

\section{Limitations}

Despite its strengths, CyberScope has some limitations:

\begin{itemize}
    \item dependency on external tools availability;
    \item performance impact when running multiple tools simultaneously;
    \item limited support for highly complex authentication mechanisms.
\end{itemize}

\section{Future Work}

Future improvements may include:

\begin{itemize}
    \item deeper AI integration for vulnerability prediction;
    \item support for authenticated scanning;
    \item cloud-based distributed scanning;
    \item improved visualization and reporting capabilities.
\end{itemize}
